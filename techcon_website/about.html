History

TechCon began over a decade ago as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts. It has since grown into one of the most anticipated annual technology conferences, attracting thousands of innovators, developers, and thought leaders worldwide.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and shape the future of technology by bringing together diverse voices and groundbreaking ideas.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted some of the most influential voices in technology:

  • Dr. Ada Lovelace – Renowned for her contributions to early computing theory and inspiring women in tech.

  • Alan Turing – Pioneer of modern computing and artificial intelligence.

  • Grace Hopper – Innovator in programming languages and champion of computer science education.
